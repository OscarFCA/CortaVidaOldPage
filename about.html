We are Corta Vida

More Than Jewelry, A Story to Wear

Corta Vida was born from a journey — from fleeting conversations, distant landscapes, and those unrepeatable instants that stay with us forever. Founded by Sofía Rodríguez Calleja, the brand reflects years of exploration across more than 22 countries, where cultures, textures, and emotions became fragments of inspiration.

Each piece is more than an accessory; it is a tangible memory, a fragment of the world transformed into wearable art. With every design, Corta Vida embraces imperfection, individuality, and the beauty of what is real.

Our Craft

Every jewel is handmade — artisanal, unique, and authentic. Minimalist in form yet bold in character, each piece carries a natural brilliance that doesn't need excess. Inspired by what is simple, real, and enduring, our designs are meant to be lived in, evolving with the person who wears them.

970 Silver

At the heart of our creations lies 970 silver — an alloy purer than traditional 925. Containing 97% fine silver and 3% copper, it is brighter, more radiant, and more enduring. This material not only elevates the shine but also enhances durability, ensuring each jewel becomes part of your story for years to come.

970 silver: purer, brighter, and more special. Handmade with love, built to last and stand out. Your shine, your story, your style.
